8. Installation on the Cytometer

Parts

  • 2 M4X30 Hex Cap
  • 2 M4 Nuts
  • 4 M3X20
  • 4 M3 nut
  • 12 M4x12 Countersunk Head

Tools

  • 1 2mm Allen key
  • 1 2.5mm Allen key

Step 1: Dilution chamber

  • Install the ChamberArm.stl like the picture below with 2 screws M4X30 Hex Cap and 2 M4 Nuts.

  • Use a Colson ring to creat a tab and insert it in the hole as shown in the picture below.

  • Put the magnetic strirring systems through the hole provided for this purpose and push it in until the end.

  • Slide the Chamber.stl on the ChamberArm.stl

Step 2: Segregostat on the Cytometer

  • Slide the Segregostat (green arrow) onto the cytometer cover by inserting it from the back using the skids (purple arrow).

  • Put the stoppers from underside and screw with M3X20 (x2) and M3 nut (x2) for each parts using a 2mm Allen key.

  • Place the Segregostat so that the stoppers are in contact with the cytometer cover.

  • Screw in the M5x120 screws (left & Right side) to tighten the Segregostat frame on the Cytometer cover. (4 screws)

Step 3: Segregostat Cover

  • Use M4x12 Countersunk Head to secure the cover to the front and back panel with an 2.5mm Allen key.
