Step 2: Electrical step for the Rear panel

  • Connect the fan to the wago + and - ( red-> (+) / blue -> (-) )

  • Connect the two power pins of the motherboard with a black wire and a red wire from the wago (+) and (-).

  • Connect the relays to de wago with a red cable for the (+) and black cable for the (-), You can make a bridge between the positive terminal of the relay that is powered and the second one. You can also make a bridge between the negative terminals.

  • You can use rislan clamps to hold the cabling in the cable management pipe for the fan and the relays.

  • Connect the two cables of the 2 D-sub 15 connectors, one cable on the COM port and the other on the NC port, repeat the same process for the second connector. Normally this step is already done.

  • Connect positive pin of the DC socket to the switch and from the switch to the wago (+) (all this process with a RED cable). Connect minus pin of the DC directely to the wago (-).

  • Connect the cable from the rs 232 connector to the motherboard following the plan.

  • Connect the INT pins of the relays to the motheboard following the plan (W12 & W14).

Step 3: Electrical step for the Front panel

  • Connect COM A / COM B / COM C / COM D to the Wago (+), you must creat bridges with RED cables between COM A/B/C/D and connect only the COM A with a RED cable to the Wago.

  • Now connect all pumps from the motherboard's relays, and connect all minus on the front panel Wago following the plan. Please Use the plan number 1 if your segregregostat is on the RIGHT of the fermentor, or the number 2 if it's on the LEFT. The only changes are in the order of the pumps, which makes it easier to place the pipes for the sampling.

  • Put a black wire into the Wago (-) which is on the back panel to the Wago (-) on the front panel.
