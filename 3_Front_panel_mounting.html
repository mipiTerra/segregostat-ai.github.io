3. Front panel mounting

Parts

  • 1 Wago Support
  • 2 M3x6
  • 1 Wago
  • 3 Head pump / 13Rpm
  • 10 M4x30
  • 2 Head pump / 290Rpm

Step 1: Fix wago support

  • Install the Wago Support on the 3D printed part FrontL.stl using M3x6 (x2) and screwing directly into the plastic.

  • Put a Wago inside the support.

Step 2: Main part

  • Put the two parts together to form the main part with the 3D printed parts FrontR.stl & FrontL.stl

  • Add the centre Anti-vibrator.stl (3D printed with Flex Filament) and screw the first Head pump / 13Rpm from the back (see second picture) with M4x30 (x2) in this way the two 3d printed parts will hold together.

Step 3: Add all pumps

  • Fix all the pump (Head pump / 13Rpm & Head pump / 290Rpm) with the anti-vibrator.stl (printed with Flex filament) using M4x30 (x8).
